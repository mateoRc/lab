# Sentinel

**Personal project** · Active, pre-1.0

A Python CI/CD release guardian that turns deterministic engineering evidence
into explainable release-risk assessments.

- Change-aware regression, security, contract, and degradation checks
- Strict policy validation and centralized evidence redaction
- Markdown, JSON, pull-request, and sanitized runtime reports
- Advisory analysis; deterministic policy retains pass/fail authority
